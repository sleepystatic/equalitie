RETURNS

RETURN POLICY

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery.

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached.

HOW TO RETURN

  1. Email us at returns@equalitie.com with your order number
  2. Pack your item securely in original packaging
  3. Include your order confirmation
  4. Ship to the address we provide

EXCHANGES

For size exchanges, please follow the return process above and place a new order for your desired size. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.

FREE SHIPPING

All orders ship free. Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the item received was defective or incorrect.
